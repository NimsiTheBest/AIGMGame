Let's make a game!
  name:the game
  by:Nimsi
Layout
   use default
Buttons
*makemoney
name:Make money
desc:Click this button to make money!
on click:yield 1 money
class:bigButton
 
Resources
*money
name:Money
desc:This is how much money you got!

Buildings
*printer
name:Printer
desc:A printer that prints money, cool right?? 1 MPS.
cost:100 money
on tick:yield 1 money
tag:build, printer
 
*laptop
name:Laptop
desc:The same 3 videos on repeat. 11 MPS.
cost:1300 money
on tick:yield 11 money
tag:build, laptop

*superprinter
name:Super Printer
desc:This printer is way faster than the older one! Generates 10 money per second
cost:1000 money
on tick:yield 10 money
req:5 printer:max
tag:build, printer, super
 
Upgrades
*printupgrade1
name:More Ink
desc:Doubles the Money yield of Printers.
cost:500 money
passive:multiply money yield of tag:printer by 2
req:2 printer

*printupgrade2
name:Printer Stacking
desc:Stacks printers to be more effective. Every 10 printers, 3x yield for all Printers.
cost:500 money
passive:
	multiply money yield of tag:printer by max(1, floor(printer / 10))
end
req:10 printer
 
*printupgrade3
name:Even More Ink
desc:2.5x money from Printers
cost:2000 money
passive:
	multiply money yield of tag:printer by 2.5
end
req:15 printer, printupgrade1

*printupgrade4
name:Computer Wiring
desc:By connecting the Laptops to the Printers, we can sync them up. Printers gain 5% MPS from Laptops and vice versa.
cost:500 money
passive:
	multiply money yield of tag:printer by laptop * 0.05
	multiply money yield of tag:laptop by printer * 0.05
end
req:15 printer

*clickupgrade1
name:Click Glove
desc:+1 money per click.
cost:50 money
req:50 money:max
passive:increase money yield of tag:bigButton by 1

*clickupgrade2
name:Metallic Fingertips
desc:+9 money per click.
cost:444 money
req:444 money:max
passive:increase money yield of tag:bigButton by 9

Shinies
*moneybox
  name:Money Box
  on click:yield (random((money:ps * 300), (money:ps * 3000))) money
  movement:anywhere
  frequency:60
